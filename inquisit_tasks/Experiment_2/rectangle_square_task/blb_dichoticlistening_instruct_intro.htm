Dichotic listening task

For this task you will have to decide which sound is being played. There will be a series of consecutive trials. On each trial, the following will happen:

  • A fixation cross will appear in the middle of the screen. Please look straight at the cross.
  • A sound will then play. It will be very brief.
  • You will then decide if the sound is "ba", "da", "ga", "ka", "pa" or "ta".

Your goal is to indicate the sound that you heard

Sometimes you might hear two sounds. If you hear two sounds, you should select the sound that was clearest.
